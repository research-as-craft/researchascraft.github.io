Research as Craft

Research as Craft is a newsletter and community about how we can grow and improve as researchers. Each issue, we share ideas, resources, tools and more to support people who do research - whether in industry, policy, academia, activism or elsewhere.

It’s inspired by the idea of traditional crafts as tangible practices that can be honed, shared and developed.

You can read more about the ethos behind RaC in our first issue, or read our previous issues for a backlist of ideas, tips, tools and more…
